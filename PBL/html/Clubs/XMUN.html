XMUN   Model United Nations


     Model United Nations -- originated in the United States as an academic simulation of the General Assembly and other multilateral institutions. Model United Nations is a civic education activity for young people in which young students take on the role of diplomatic representatives of different countries or other political entities in meetings on hot international issues. Sit still, visualise, youth, the world. Dialogue with the world, but also with the growth of dialogue. The Model United Nations Club of Xishan School Affiliated to Renmin University of China was established in 2016. With a view to popularizing the rules and spirit of Model United Nations, it aims to drive more teenagers to participate in Model United Nations activities and enhance their international vision.
