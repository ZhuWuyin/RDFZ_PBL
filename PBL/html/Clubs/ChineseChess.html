Chinese Chess


     Chinese chess can be said to be one of the quintessence of China. Chinese chess has a broad mass base, players through the game test each other, so that their thinking careful, logical reasoning ability is greatly strengthened. Both sides come and go, each side does not give way, the superior side should grasp the situation, do not rush to avoid the possibility of the other side upset; The inferior side needs to lay down the mind, never give up, seize the loopholes of the other side and actively play the law, try every means to disturb the situation and win in the chaos. Playing chess is not only a competition of strength, but also a contest of mentality, and a comprehensive reflection of the wills and qualities of both sides.
