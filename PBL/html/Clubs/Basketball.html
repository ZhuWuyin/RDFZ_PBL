Basketball


     The basketball club is a sports and entertainment club that includes everything about basketball. Whether you watch basketball or play basketball, whether you know a little about basketball or have never played it, if you care about basketball, love the game or just have an interest in it, the basketball club is ready to welcome you. With the gradual development of the basketball club, we will carry out a series of related activities, so that students can gain more fun in practice. While carrying out training activities, we will understand the potential members through each activity and answer the questions and doubts of the members who are not strong enough. We hope that our efforts can provide a good foundation and guarantee for the development of the club.
